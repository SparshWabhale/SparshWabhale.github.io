Coral Reef Prediction and Analysis

Project information

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Project date:November, 2023
  • Project URL: Coral Reef Prediction and Analysis

This research stands out from previous research by introducing Dynamic Coral Taxa Prediction, utilizing time series analysis and deep learning to forecast changes in coral taxa over time, considering environmental variables such as sea surface temperature and human impacts. Unlike prior studies, our work features a comprehensive AI-based framework that incorporates machine learning and explainable AI to classify coral health. The diversity of algorithms explored, including Random Forest, LSTM, and Simplified Feed Forward Neural Network, enhances the methodological robustness of our research. By providing a holistic perspective on coral reef conservation that actively predicts and mitigates future threats.
